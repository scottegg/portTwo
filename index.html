About

Hello, thank you for stopping by! I'm a newly minted coding nerd looking to do some big things for a company looking to do the same. My coding journey started back in the summer looking for ways to utilize big data and how to compile it. As I learned after completing some Python challenges, I needed some in person help. That led me to joining Tech Talent South in October. It was quite a leap to completely leave everything I had known to enter this developer realm. A few short months later, I couldn't be happier. I'm coding constantly and learning new ways to do things. I hope to become a coding genius before too long.

Before all this coding, I was a sales engineer for four years and a property insurance adjuster just before that. My glory days were spent at Clemson University where I majored in Ceramics and Materials Engineering. I love my Tigers and a lot of my time is spent at football games. While I call Charlotte home now, I grew up in a small town called Bluefield, VA. If you know where that is, I'll be very impressed.

To give you a deeper dive into my life, I'll give you a few fun facts. I love television and movies. You'll hear me spitting out quotes left and right. I've been told it's a problem, but I don't see it. I use exercise as damage control for my love of tasty treats. I volunteer for Make-A-Wish Foundation here in Charlotte and it's a blast! Those are some teaser facts. If you want to learn anything else, feel free to ask.
